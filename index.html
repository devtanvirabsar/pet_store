Frequently Asked Questions!

What types of pets do you sell?
At Pet Store, we offer a variety of pets, including dogs, cats, birds, rabbits, and small reptiles. We ensure that all our pets are healthy and well-cared for before they find their new homes.
Do you provide pet food and accessories?
Yes! We have a wide range of pet food, toys, grooming products, and accessories to keep your pet happy and healthy.
Can I adopt a pet instead of buying one?
Absolutely! We support pet adoption and often collaborate with local shelters to help abandoned animals find loving homes. Check our Adoption Section for available pets.
Do you offer pet grooming and veterinary services?
Yes, we provide professional pet grooming services, including bathing, hair trimming, and nail clipping. We also have experienced veterinarians available for regular check-ups and vaccinations.
What is your return or exchange policy for pets?
We prioritize the well-being of our pets. If you experience any health-related concerns within 7 days of purchase, you can contact us for assistance. However, we do not support returns due to personal preference changes.
